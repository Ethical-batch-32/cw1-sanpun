OUR SERVICES

Nail Trimming

Nail trimming is a major part of dog grooming services. A dog’s nails grow and if not cut properly or timely, they can grow wildly and curl. This becomes painful for the dogs and may even pierce the dog’s paws. Such incidents can create further painful infections for the dogs. Nail trimming is done by a nail clipper. However, an inexperienced can cause pain for the dog while using it. Hence, it is better to opt for professional nail clipping to keep your dog calm and at ease.

Dog Bath

Love to see your dog clean but don’t have the time or the backyard for such dog grooming service? Pawgroom's dog grooming in Kathmandu offers the most luscious baths for the biggest of dogs. Haircuts

We love to spice up our look but ever thought your dog could rock a cut of its own? Well, Pawgroom’s partners have come up with incredibly amazing haircuts for all types of dogs. Haircuts aren’t just stylish but a proper way to care for your pet’s fur. Dog’s like Shi Tzus require such pet grooming service so that they can see better. Other dogs like spaniels, huskies and lhasa apso also need timely haircuts. Style it in your own personalized tastes and watch your dog transform from just a ball of fur to a high class cutie!

Hand Stripping

Hand stripping is a rare dog grooming service in Kathmandu but required nonetheless. It consists of pulling out dead hair out of the coat to give room for newer stronger hair to grow. The process is done carefully by skillful fingers of the professional groomers. It is the best type of dog grooming service for furry non-shedding dogs.Style your dog and transform from just a ball of fur to a beauty!
